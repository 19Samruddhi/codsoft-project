Doodhpathri

Doodhpathi or the valley of milk, is a beautiful lush verdant about 42 kms from Srinagar at an altitude of 2730m above the sea level in south-west of Kashmir valley. Having remained in oblivion for centuries and known to shepherds and trekkers only, this valley is a recent discovery in the tourist map of Kashmir.
